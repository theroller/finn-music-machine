**Who**: Dad + Claude
**Goal**: Have a working prototype ready before Finn sits down.

**Built**:
- Single-file `index.html` with Tone.js via CDN
- Four preset sequences (π, Fibonacci, primes, phone)
- Six scales (pentatonic, major, minor, blues, Japanese, chromatic)
- Tempo slider (60–480 bpm) and octave-range slider (1–4)

**Notes for the first session with Finn**:

- Have him try his own sequence: his birthday digits, his Roblox username typed as numbers, whatever.
- Ask him: "If you could change one thing about this, what would it be?" — feeds Week 2.
- Save his favorite combo (sequence + scale + tempo) at the end of the session.
